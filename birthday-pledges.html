Birthday Pledges

Birthdays are a special time to celebrate the goodness of God in our lives. The Bible tells us in Psalm 65:11 “You crown the year with your goodness; your paths overflow with rich food,”

World Vision Tanzania would like to invite you to pledge your birthday to share the goodness of God by helping us reach more vulnerable children. All you will need to do is to use your preferred social media platform to recruit your friends and family to donate any amount to the Mtoto Kwanza Fund during your birthday week.

So far, your birthday pledges have enabled us to raise 1 Million Tanzania Shillings. Because you celebrated your birthday by giving back, lives continue to be impacted:

  • 2,550 school-going children supported with learning materials, school uniforms, and sanitary pads for girls.
  • 2,350 households and children reached with piped water and water purification technology.

Imagine how many more children can be supported through your birthday.
