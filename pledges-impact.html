Birthday Pledge Impact

World Vision Tanzania endevours to help children get a good education and live their lives to the fullest.

The smiles of these children hide stories of struggle, suffering, and in some cases despair. Many come from broken homes, are orphaned, abused, go to bed hungry on most days and some live in the streets with no place to call home.

By pledging your birthday, you are helping to create awareness about these children and raising the funds needed to transform their lives for good. The funds raised will go towards providing school kits and uniforms, better water and sanitation in the schools, trauma counselling, and food to stay in school.

Select your birthday month below and see the faces of the children that are waiting on Tanzanians like you to pledge and give them hope for a brighter future.
